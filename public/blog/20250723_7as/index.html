Seven arcsecond seeing

2025-07-23

We just came out of an observing run with the IRTF ASM. The weather… wasn’t great… but we were able to obtain useful data. One night Subaru reported 7" seeing.

Our 12x12 Shack-Hartmann wavefront sensor looked like wind blowing through a field of wheat.

For reference, the subapertures are 4.5" across and the spots usually look like this:

I would share focal plane data but on-target images look more like flat fields. Some conditions are so miserable that they wrap around to being spectacular!
